Pizza

Description

Crispy dough spread with red tomato sauce and covered in melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (store-bought)
  • Grated mozzarella cheese
  • Grated parmesan cheese
  • Tomato-based pizza sauce (store-bought)
  • Optional toppings, such as vegetables, pepperoni, olive oil, etc.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 475 degrees F.
  2. Flatten pizza dough.
  3. Spread pizza sauce onto the dough.
  4. Cover with grated mozzarella and parmesan cheeses.
  5. Add toppings if desired.
  6. Cook pizza for 10 to 15 minutes, until golden.

Peanut Butter and Banana Sandwich

Description

Peanut butter and bananas grilled between two buttery pieces of bread.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Peanut butter
  • Banana

Steps

  1. Place both pieces of bread next to each other.
  2. Spread peanut butter on both pieces.
  3. Slice banana and place slices on one piece of bread.
  4. Combine the bread into a sandwich.
  5. Melt butter in a pan on the stove and toast the sandwich on both sides.

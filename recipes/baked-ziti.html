Baked Ziti

Description

Ziti noodles mixed with tomato sauce with ground beef and ricotta. Baked with mozzarella cheese on top.

Ingredients

  • Ziti noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Boil ziti noodles in salted water.
  3. To a baking dish, combine ziti, ricotta, tomato sauce, and raw ground beef.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over top.
  5. Bake 25-30 minutes until golden.
